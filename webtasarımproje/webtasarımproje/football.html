Welcome to Football Branch.

The football department of Fenerbahçe Sports Club, established in 1907 in Istanbul, is one of Turkey's most rooted and successful football teams. The team, with its yellow and navy-blue colors, has achieved significant success in the Süper Lig, Turkish Cup, and European competitions. As of 2025, it has won 28 Süper Lig championships. The team plays its home matches at the Şükrü Saracoğlu Stadium in Kadıköy.
