The Fenerbahçe Women's Volleyball Team, founded in 1954, is one of the pioneering clubs in Turkish volleyball. With over 70 years of experience, the team has achieved great success both nationally and internationally. In 2010, they won the FIVB Women's Club World Championship, claiming the world title, and in the 2011-2012 season, they became champions of the CEV Champions League. Additionally, they have won the Sultanlar League 7 times, the Turkish Cup 4 times, and the Turkish Super Cup 5 times. They play their matches at the Burhan Felek Volleyball Hall in Istanbul.
